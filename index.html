About

Tropico Photo is the collaborative work of Forrest Aguar and Michelle Norris. The duo met while studying photography at the University of Georgia and have since transplanted to Atlanta, where they live with their dog Taco.

Forrest loves pairing shapes and lines to create captivating compositions and Michelle loves seeking out compelling color combinations. Together, they make work that draws on the feeling of an ambiguous time and place.

Travel and art are their greatest shared loves.
